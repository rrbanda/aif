# AI Factory Program Charter

## Program Overview

**Program Name**: Example Bank Corp AI Factory Program
**Sponsor**: Jane Smith, Chief AI Officer
**Program Lead**: Tom Chen, VP ML Engineering
**Red Hat Field CTO**: David Kim
**Start Date**: January 15, 2026
**Target Duration**: 9 months

## Vision

Establish a systematic, scalable AI Factory that enables Example Bank to build, deploy, and operate AI models at enterprise scale, reducing time-to-production from 12 months to 8 weeks.

## Objectives

1. Deploy a production-grade AI/ML platform on OpenShift with automated MLOps pipelines
2. Deliver 3 qualified AI use cases through the full lifecycle from experiment to production
3. Build internal AI competency through structured enablement (COE, bootcamps, workshops)
4. Establish model governance framework compliant with SR 11-7 and internal risk policies
5. Achieve measurable business value from at least 1 production AI use case within 6 months

## Scope

### In Scope

- OpenShift AI platform optimization and automation
- Model lifecycle management (experiment to production to monitoring)
- 3 priority use cases: Fraud Detection, AML Alert Triage, Credit Risk Augmentation
- Team enablement: bootcamps, workshops, community of practice
- Governance framework: model risk, bias testing, security scanning

### Out of Scope

- Cloud migration (platform remains on-premise)
- Non-AI/ML workloads on OpenShift
- Third-party SaaS AI tools procurement

## Success Criteria

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Model deployment time | < 8 weeks |
| Models in production | >= 2 |
| Team members trained | >= 15 |
| Use cases qualified | >= 3 |
| False positive reduction (fraud) | >= 60% |

## Governance

- **AI Steering Committee**: Quarterly reviews with executive sponsors
- **Program Reviews**: Bi-weekly with Red Hat and internal leads
- **Value Gates**: Phase completion requires documented gate review

## Phases

1. Discovery (Completed)
2. Data Strategy (Completed)
3. Platform Foundation (In Progress)
4. Use Case Delivery
5. Scale & Optimize
6. Operate & Evolve
