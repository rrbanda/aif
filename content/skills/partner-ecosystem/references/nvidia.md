# NVIDIA Partnership for AI Factory

## NVIDIA AI Enterprise Licensing

- **Scope**: Enterprise support and certification for AI software stack on supported platforms (VMware, Red Hat OpenShift).
- **Includes**: CUDA, cuDNN, TensorRT, RAPIDS, and other NVIDIA libraries. Security updates and patches.
- **Support**: NVIDIA enterprise support for AI software; complements Red Hat support for OpenShift.
- **Use case**: Customers who want certified, supported AI stack with predictable licensing. Often required for production deployments.

## NIM (NVIDIA Inference Microservices)

- **Purpose**: Pre-built, optimized containers for serving popular foundation models (Llama, Mistral, etc.).
- **Benefits**: No need to build custom serving containers; NVIDIA-optimized for performance. Drop-in deployment.
- **Integration**: NIMs can run on OpenShift; deploy as pods with GPU resources. Compatible with Model-as-a-Service patterns.
- **Models**: Catalog of NIMs for various models and sizes. Updated as new models release.

## NeMo Framework

- **NeMo**: NVIDIA's framework for training and customizing LLMs. Supports fine-tuning, RLHF, and custom model development.
- **NeMo Guardrails**: Add guardrails to LLM outputs—safety filters, topic restrictions, fact-checking, PII redaction.
- **Use case**: Customers building custom models or needing strong guardrails for regulated or customer-facing applications.
- **Integration**: NeMo and Guardrails can run on OpenShift; use GPU Operator for scheduling.

## Joint Go-to-Market

- **Red Hat + NVIDIA**: Strategic partnership for enterprise AI. OpenShift AI is certified with NVIDIA GPUs and software.
- **Reference architectures**: Joint reference architectures for AI Factory on OpenShift with NVIDIA stack.
- **Co-selling**: Red Hat and NVIDIA sales align on opportunities; partner incentives for joint deals.
- **Events and enablement**: Joint webinars, workshops, and certification paths for partners and customers.
